We developed a new hardware-efficient algorithm for evolutionary strategies, making it many orders of magnitude faster than OpenES and 3 times faster than RL. In fact, we bring the barrier of fine-tuning down to the level of inference; i.e. if you can do (batch) inference of a system and evaluate the fitness of the system, you can optimize it with our method with only a 10 to 20% slowdown and no extra VRAM. This makes it an extremely practical method for general ML training, LLM fine-tuning, and optimizing effectively any end-to-end system of neural networks.
